About the Movement

The Common Man Uprising

Bihar has endured decades of neglect, corruption, and divisive politics. "We The People of Patna" rises as a collective effort of citizens determined to bring change from the ground up — starting with our city, Patna.

Our goal is to unite citizens beyond caste, religion, and class — to rebuild a Bihar that is just, self-reliant, and governed by people who care. We challenge the system's complacency and push for governance reforms that prioritize dignity, transparency, and equality.

As a movement, we begin by addressing the issues closest to us — reforming local governance, ensuring fair taxation, and building a civic culture of responsibility and participation.

Our Vision

Rebuilding Bihar Through People's Power

Abolish Challan Exploitation

Reforming the challan system of Patna Traffic Police to end misuse and restore fairness. Our first pilot towards a citizen-accountable governance model.

One Nation, One Tax

Introducing a 1% Banking Transaction Tax to replace multiple taxes, ensuring simplicity, transparency, and equitable revenue growth.

Strengthen Local Governance

Promoting Urban Local Self-Governance — empowering municipalities, taxpayers, and citizens to make Patna a model of participatory democracy.

Citizen Awareness Drives

Consistent mass awareness campaigns encouraging civic participation, accountability, and responsible citizenship.

Grassroots Empowerment

Mobilizing youth, women, and community groups to lead reforms at the neighborhood and ward levels for sustainable change.

Justice Through Unity

Ensuring that people reclaim their voice and collectively question authorities to demand fair, humane, and people-driven governance.

Our Ongoing Work

Initiatives & Campaigns

Patna Traffic Reform

Citizen-led petition and dialogue with authorities to replace punitive challan practices with transparent, community-centered enforcement.

Urban Self-Governance Pilot

Testing decentralized decision-making through ward-level committees involving local taxpayers and citizens.

Youth for Bihar

Mobilizing students and young professionals to participate in civic audits, data collection, and advocacy for better public service delivery.
